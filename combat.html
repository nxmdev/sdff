Combat

Highest stamina attacks first.
Attacks cost 2 stamina and defense moves cost 1 stamina. Attackers can attack until their stamina is used up or they elect to pass(reserve energy).
A combatant's stamina regenerates to full at the beginning of their attack phase.
There is an area in the top right of the character sheet to track your stamina during a battle.

The Attacker uses one of their offensive abilities to attack the Defender. Each skill, offensive or defensive, gains 1 bubble of experience for each use. When the Attacker declares an attack, they also declare which body part of the Defender they are targetting. There are vital, core, action, and movement body parts. To identify a particular body part, check the character sheet or mob sheet. Monsters and unusual mobs will identify body parts.
Head (vital body part) -2 attack roll
Chest (core body part) -1 attack roll
Arms (action body part) no penalty
Legs (movement body part) no penalty


The Defender then uses a defensive skill to avoid damage.
Attack roll is 2d6 + offensive skill modifier + dexterity statistic modifier
vs
Defense roll of 2d6 + defensive skill modifier + agility statistic modifier

If the Defender wins, no damage is taken and the attack phase continues.
The same Attacker may spend more stamina to perform another attack if they wish
or save their remaining stamina to perform defensive manuevers when the attack phase swaps to their opponent.
Mobs will stop attacking once their stamina reaches 3 or lower unless otherwise stated on their character sheet.

If the attack is successful, damage is then calculated against the Defender's armor.
Strength + weapon damage
vs
Agility + armor rating

If the Defender matches or exceeds the Attackers roll, damage is mitigated by the armor and no wounds are recieved.
If the damage exceeds the armor roll, then the Defender's body part takes 1 wound from single handed weapons and 2 wounds from two handed weapons.

If the Defender ever runs out of stamina, they automatically take the hit. Once the Attacker runs out of stamina or they elect to pass turn, it is now the Defender's turn to be the Attacker and use their stamina to initiate attacks against the Attacker now turned Defender.

The combatants take turns attacking until any 2 body parts are fully wounded(incapacitated) or 1 vital body part has been incapacitated, generally your chest and head. Check character sheet for details on body parts for monsters and other unusual mobs.
