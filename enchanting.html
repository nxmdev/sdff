Enchanting

Enchanting is offered by merchants of the Wizard guild. The enchantment usually costs coin plus an item found in the world or dropped from a mob (check mob sheets for loot info). These enchantments are very powerful and can make even the weakest item worth wielding.
Applied Item category will list what item type the enchant can be applied to:
(weapon type) / (armor type)
