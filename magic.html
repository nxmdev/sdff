Magic Combat


Magic offensive spells cost 2 stamina, the same as melee attacks.

Magic defensive spells cost 1 stamina, the same as melee defense.

When calculating the damage of a spell vs armor rating, use INT + Spell Damage.

To use higher tier spells you must meet the requirements stated in the spells description.

Every third level your character has the ability to learn 1 spell if they choose. Note these on your character sheet.

Using a magic tome allows a spell caster to scribe more spells and access them in combat. To use a tome you must have it equiped in one of your hands. A character may scribe one spell per level in a magic tome. Example, a level 3 wizard would have 3 spells scribed in a magic tome and 1 assigned to his character regardless of the tome being equiped. Note these spells on your character sheet.
