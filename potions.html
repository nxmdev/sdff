Potions


Potions are very powerful magic that are held in
unbreakable magic glass containers attached onto a belt and drank to gain magical effects.

Potion Belt

A potion belt can hold up to 3 potions at once. The belt does not specifically have to be worn around your waist but must be worn or carried to be used in combat. The potion belt can be restocked outside of combat at anytime or during combat for the cost of 2 stamina per potion and only during your attack phase.
