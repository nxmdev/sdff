Combat

In combat you will take turns using your stamina to perform attacks and defensive manuevers. Attacks cost 2 stamina and defensive moves cost 1 stamina. Stamina regenerates to its full amount at the beginning of each combatants attack phase. Your stamina is 5 + (stamina modifier).
There is a box in the top right of the character sheet to provide you with a place to track your stamina during a battle.

The combatant with the highest stamina goes first. The attacker can use one of their offensive abilities to attack the other combatant. The defending combatant then uses a defensive skill to avoid damage.

Attack roll is 2d6 + offensive skill modifier + dexterity statistic modifier
compared against
Defense roll of 2d6 + defensive skill modifier + agility statistic modifier
