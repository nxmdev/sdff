Abilities

There are three types of Abilities: Offensive, Defensive, and Class. Offensive and Defensive Abilities gains one bubble of experience each time they are used. Fill the experience bubbles and upon filling the 10th bubble you gain a level in that ability. Ability levels are used as modifiers to your attack and defense rolls when that ability is being used.
Class Abilities do not level, can be combat or non-combat, and are not tied to equipment. Choose one Class ability when you create your character and one each time you level up.

· Block - An ability given to the character when a shield is equiped. If successful, blocks all incoming damage from the attack it targetted.

· Crush - An ability given to the character when a one handed mace is equiped. Crush applies 1 wound when successful.

· Dodge - An ability that all characters have innately regardless of gear. Dodge cannot be used if one of your legs(movement wound) has been fully wounded and incapacitated. Dodge rolls can be heavily altered by using heavy armor. Chainmail(-1), Leather(-1), and Platemail(-2) will lower your dodge roll by the specified amount. This negative modifier is only applied once and is equal to the highest tier armor you are wearing.
Examples:
1. If you have 1 piece of Leather, you now apply -1 to your dodge roll.
2. If you have 1 piece of Platemail, you now apply -2 to your dodge roll.
3. If you have 2 pieces of Platemail, you still only apply a -2 to your dodge roll.
4. If you have 1 piece of Leather and 2 pieces of Platemail, you still only apply -2 to your dodge roll.

· Dual Wield - An ability that grants the character the use of 2 one handed weapons. The consequence of this is allowing the attacker to take the highest of the 2d6 attack rolls and use the highest value of the two for both of them. Also this applies both weapon's damage values when the damage is calculated.

· Feet Like Cat - Once each combat, you turn a failed dodge into a successful dodge.

· Glancing Blow - Once each combat, take 1 less wound from an attack.

· Heavy Strike - Once each combat, add 1 wound to a successful attack.

· Pierce - An ability given to the character when a dagger is equiped. Pierce applies 1 wound when successful.

· Retalitory Strike - Once each combat, when you make a successful defense roll you attack back inflicting 1 wound to a random body part. Roll a 1d6.
1-Left Leg, 2-Right Leg, 3-Left Arm, 4-Right Arm, 5-Chest, 6-Head

· Shurikens - Pre-combat, you throw a shuriken from the shadows, applying 1 wound to any non-vital body part.

· Slash - An ability given to the character when a one handed sword is equiped. Slash applies 1 wound when successful.

· Slice - An ability given to the character when a two handed sword is equiped. Slice applies 2 wounds when successful.

· Smash - An ability given to the character when a two handed maul is equiped. Smash applies 2 wounds when successful.

· Stab - An ability given to the character when a two handed polearm is equiped. Stab applies 2 wounds when successful.
