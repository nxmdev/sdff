Death Effects

When your character dies in The Realm you are reborn anew at The Welcoming Chamber, however, a piece of your past will always come with you. When you die roll 1d100 and apply the listed Death Effect at the bottom of your character sheet.

As an alternative way to play, when you die, choose your death effect from the list. Random death effect generation is meant to deter dying on purpose to stack certain death effects, such as a transformation or immunity effects, but this is a completely legitamite way to play. If you are playing a narrative or story heavy game, then it is preferred to choose your death effect to help tailor it to the way you died.
