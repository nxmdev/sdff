Statistics

When creating a new character you will start with 10 stat points. Stats will influence your character in the following ways:

· STRENGTH - Increases MELEE weapon damage.
· INTELLIGENCE - Increases SPELL damage.
· DEXTERITY - Increases RANGE weapon damage. Also determines weapon or spell accuracy to hit.
· AGILITY - Increases chance for defensive abilities to be successful. Also increases effectiveness of armor.
· STAMINA - Increases stamnia pool, used to perform attacks and defensive manuevers.
